COMMUNAL COFFEE

I’m pretty basic when it comes to drinking coffee. I go to Starbucks and always pick up my favorite vanilla latte or I sometimes get a tea lemonade but it doesn’t change from that.

Since this can get really repetitive and boring, I decided to branch out and try a new coffee shop at least once a week or twice a month, depending on my schedule.

This week, I decided to try out COMMUNAL COFFEE.

They have outdoor and indoor seating, which is beautifully set-up.

I grabbed a Vanilla Bean latte and it was great! It satisfied my taste buds. Highly recommend checking it out if you’re in the area!
